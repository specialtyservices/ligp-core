Unless otherwise stated, the protocol name, structure, and formal definitions constitute protected intellectual property of the author.

Permitted Use (Default License)
Subject to the terms below, LIGP may be used for:
educational and research purposes,
internal organizational governance,
policy drafting and AI usage standards,
non-commercial professional practice.

Attribution Requirement
Any reuse, adaptation, or reference shall include clear attribution in the following form:
“Based on the LLM Interaction Governance Protocol (LIGP) developed by [Author / Organization].”
Attribution must be visible in:
documentation,
training materials,
policy annexes,
or published analyses.



Restrictions
The following uses are not permitted without explicit written consent:
Commercial resale of LIGP as a standalone product
Rebranding the protocol under a different name
Claiming authorship or exclusive ownership
Incorporation into proprietary frameworks without attribution
Marketing claims implying endorsement by the original author

Derivative Works
Derivative works are permitted provided that:
the original protocol is clearly credited,
modifications are explicitly identified,
no claim is made that the derivative is the original LIGP.
Suggested phrasing:
“This implementation is derived from, but not identical to, the LLM Interaction Governance Protocol (LIGP).”

“LLM Interaction Governance Protocol” and “LIGP” may be treated as:
a named methodology,

or a framework identifier.
Trademark registration (if pursued) does not alter the attribution requirements above.
